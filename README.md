Campus Connect is a unified mentorship platform designed exclusively for USF students. It bridges the gap between batches by making mentorship accessible, less awkward, and more effective.

Juniors often feel intimidated to approach seniors for guidance. Campus Connect solves this by providing a dedicated space where everyone is a mentor to someone below their year.
